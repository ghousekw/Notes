How to login into Linux
What SSH is
Which SSh clients to use

Connecting Directly:
  Open the terminal in GUI:
    Just enter username and password to login
  Open the terminal in CLI:
    Terminal will be displayed then, just enter your username and password to login

Connecting Over the Network:
----------------------------
. SSH(Secure Shell)
. Windows - PuTTY
    -Open PuTTy and enter hostname/ipaddress to login
. Mac - Terminal/sshcommand line utilty
    -open terminal
    -ssh hostname/ipaddress
    -ssh username@linuxservername/ipaddress
      ex:ssh adminuser@linuxsvr
      ex:ssh linuxsvr.yourcompany.com
      ex: ssh 10.0.0.4
